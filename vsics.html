About Us

Dr. Virendra Swarup Educational Foundation, Uttar Pradesh.(VSEF) was established in the year 1989 in the memory of Late Dr. Virendra Swarup, MLC & Chairman of Uttar Pradesh Legislative Council and an eminent educationalist.

VSICS is the first self-financed institute in Kanpur to impart computer education in the form of MCA. VSICS in a span of more than 22 years has gained immense recognition as a center of excellence. Its MCA, MBA, BCA & BBA programmes have been designed to create the best talent in the IT & Management field.
